# W21-1 — Reading-F experimental test (KEYSTONE)

Reading-F INVERSION REFUTED on n=2 smoke. v2_rate (Reading E) confirmed
as dominant (+6.91pp recovery). Disabling Python's stability multiplier
does NOT close the residual gap; combined with v2_rate it actively
hurts (-3.21pp).

Implication: Python's stability acts as a useful regularizer for
v2_rate-activated anticipative solutions. Reading F is an interaction
effect, not a simple gap-closer.

W21-5 ablation matrix needs V5/V6/V7 implementations to pin down the
residual.
