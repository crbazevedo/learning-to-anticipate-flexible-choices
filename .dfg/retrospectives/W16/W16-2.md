  the existing pattern uses dataclass with mutable __post_init__

  portfolios this is 87 * 5 = 435 comparisons per evaluation — fine
  for population sizes ≤ 100, but if W18 ramps to 30-seed × 24-period
  × 200-MC the constant matters. Could precompute a "bracket lookup"
